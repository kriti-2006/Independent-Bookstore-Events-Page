    How a reservation comes together

    1. 01

      Find the listing

      Search by title, author or day, or narrow things down with a category chip. Sort by soonest, latest, title or seats remaining.

    2. 02

      Open the details

      Click any card to see the full description, host, location and how many seats remain before committing.

    3. 03

      Reserve a seat

      Fill in name, email and seat count. Bad entries are flagged in place — nothing submits until the form is clean.

    4. 04

      Receive confirmation

      A confirmation appears immediately, and the seat count updates for the next visitor browsing the same listing.
